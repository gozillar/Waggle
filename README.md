# Waggle Device Performance Analysis
Product Comparison & Executive Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project presents a boardroom-ready Power BI report developed for Waggle, a startup that designs smart fitness devices for pets. Following the success of Waggleâ€™s Lapdog fitness collar for dogs, the company is considering launching a Lapcat version for cats.
To support this decision, Waggle distributed 1,000 Lapcat prototypes and collected months of usage, rating, pet, and family demographic data. My role as a Business Intelligence Analyst was to analyze this data and deliver a clear, visually compelling executive report comparing Lapcat performance to the established Lapdog product.

The final output helps leadership determine whether Lapcat is a viable next product or a costly risk.

## Business Questions
The analysis focused on two primary executive-level questions:
1. Did average daily steps increase for cats wearing Lapcat devices as they did for dogs wearing Lapdog devices?
2. Were Lapcat owners as satisfied with the product as Lapdog owners?

Additional objectives included:
- Comparing pet demographics (species, breed, age, gender, weight)
- Analyzing family demographics (location, income, household size)
- Designing an on-brand, interactive Power BI report suitable for executive presentation
